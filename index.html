Yuwei Zhang

Hi, I am Yuwei (Evelyn) Zhang, a third-year PhD student in the Mobile Systems Research Lab at the University of Cambridge, supervised by Prof. Cecilia Mascolo. I am a member of Jesus College and a Cambridge Trust Scholar. My research is jointly supported by Nokia Corporation and Cambridge Trust. I am currently also a student researcher at Google Health.

I am generally interested in machine learning for mobile health monitoring, and my current research focuses on generalizable pretrained deep learning models and multimodal LLMs for mobile and wearable health sensing. Previously, as a MPhil student at Clare Hall, I explored uncertainty-aware federated learning for trustworthy healthcare applications.

I received my bachelor degree from the School of Computer Science at Fudan University, where I have been very fortunate to work closely with Prof. Yang Chen on social computing.
